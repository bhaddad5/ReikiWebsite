About Reiki

Animal reiki is a compassionate, meditative space that allows for healing. It is non-invasive, yet still very powerful, and can help heal physical, emotional, and spiritual problems an animal may be going through.
Furever Reiki follows the Let Animals Lead Approach, developed by Kathleen Prasaad, as well as the Animal Reiki Practitioner Code of Ethics. This approach was founded upon traditional Japanese reiki techniques and principals, but with a few differences to allow for animals. First and foremost, animals are viewed as equal partners in the healing process, and nothing is done without their consent. In traditional human reiki, touch is common; however, in animal reiki, touch is not used unless the animal seeks it out. Finally, permission is always asked of them before a session is started, and the healing space is there for them to join if they so choose. It is not done to them, it is done with them.

About Me

Deanna has had a lifelong passion for animals. Starting at a young age, she began volunteering at a local shelter, and as she got older her love for them continued to grow. With aspirations to become a veterinarian, she started college in 2014 only to leave due to chronic health issues a year and a half later. After a long battle with her health, she finally found her footing again, and became a professional pet sitter with a small company called Peticularly Perfect. That was when she also began to explore the world of animal reiki. In mid-2019, she got her certification after studying with the renowned animal reiki practitioner Kathleen Prasaad.

Peticularly Perfect

Peticularly Perfect is a small pet sitting company based out of Escondido, California that serves almost all of North County. Deanna became a professional pet sitter with them in November of 2018, and has since partnered Furever Reiki with them. They provide dog walking services, overnight stays, twenty-four hour in-home pet care, pet boarding, and dog training.

Booking

Animals are always most comfortable in their own homes, and comfort is a big part of the healing process. Therefore, Furever Reiki sessions exclusively take place in client homes. The duration and frequency of visits all depend on the needs of the individual pet.
Furever Reiki uses an external system called Pet Sitting Plus (PSP) for billing, scheduling, and client information. This system is used in conjunction with Peticularly Perfect, and their services as well as Furever Reiki’s can be obtained there.
